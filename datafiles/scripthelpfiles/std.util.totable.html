totable

Converts a given iteratable container to Lua Table.

 

Syntax:

totable(container=, Force1D=false)

 

From Vector

>>v=std.rand(3)
>>v
0.0238247     0.656005     0.879824     COL

>>std.totable(v)
0.0238247     0.656005     0.879824

 

From Array

>>type(arr)
Array
>>arr
"Treatment"     24     43     58

>>std.totable(arr)
Treatment     24     43     58

 

From Matrix

>>m=std.rand(2, 2)
>>m
0.77254     0.39515
0.77577     0.24375

>>std.totable(m, true)
0.77254     0.395152     0.775774     0.243749

 

>>t=std.totable(m)
>>t
table     table

>>t[1][1]
0.77254
