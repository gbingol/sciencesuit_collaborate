polyfit

Performs nth degree polynomial (anxn + an-1xn-1 +..+ a0)
curve fitting on a given dataset.

Syntax:

polyfit(x=,y=,n=, [intercept=]) → Vector
polyfit{x=,y=,n=, [intercept=]} → Vector

where x and y are Vectors and n is the degree of the polynomial.
If intercept is provided, then the other coefficients will be calculated based on the given intercept.
The function returns a vector containing the coefficients of the fitting.

 

Examples:

x y
0 4
1 10
2 26
3 58
4 112
5 194

 

>>coef=std.polyfit(x, y, 3)
>>coef
1    2    3    4    COL

corresponding to the polynomial: y=x3+2x2+3x+4
