frexp

Returns m and n such that x = m × 2n.
n is an integer and the absolute value of m is in the range [0.5, 1) (or zero when x is zero).

Syntax:

frexp(x) → number, integer

Example Usage:

>>math.frexp(3)
0.75    2

>>math.frexp(32)
0.5    6

Note:

As seen in the above examples, n is such that 2n >x.
i.e., x=32 ⇒ n=6, since 26=64
Then m=x/2e (i.e. 0.5=32/64)
