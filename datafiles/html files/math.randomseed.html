randomseed

Sets x as the "seed" for the pseudo-random generator
Note that equal seeds produce equal sequences of numbers.

Syntax:

randomseed(x)

Example Usage:

>>math.randomseed(10)

>>math.random()
0.515717
>>math.random()
0.0998535

--let's use the same seed (compare the generated sequence)

>>math.randomseed(10) --same seed

>>math.random()
0.515717
>>math.random()
0.0998535
